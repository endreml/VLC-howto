Motivation

What is VLC

VLC stands out from other media players because of the wide range of uses. One can use VLC to convert video and audio file types and synchronize video, audio and subtitles. It offers a variety of playback options and options to personalize the environment to use VLC effectively. VLC is available for all OS’s and has the same features in all OS’s.

VLC can record input from peripheral equipment such as a microphone and webcam. This recording function allows for very simple video editing by recording snippets of a video, storing them and sequencing the pieces in the order you need. Knowledge about the default keybinds is transferable to other software of similar characteristics.

Prerequisites

The instructions are written for users with a basic knowledge of the operating system, and a decent understanding of the English language. We suggest Firefox, Chrome or Safari browser, as this guide does not support Internett Explorer or Edge.
